Here is what the recipe calls for

    Ingredients
  • 6 eggs
  • 2 Cups of Flour
  • 2 Tablespoons of Vanilla Extract
  • 2 Teaspoons of baking powder
  • 1 Cup of sugar
    Instructions
  1. Add eggs, flour and vanilla extract and mix.
  2. Combine with sugar and baking powder and stir
  3. Eat half the cookie batter because it's delicious
  4. Take the rest of the bookie batter and make cookies
  5. Bake at 350 for 25 minutes
  6. Enjoy!
